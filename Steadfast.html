ABOUT US

Steadfast is a courier owned and operated, same-day delivery service based in Washington DC, serving the DMV region.


We serve clients in the legal, architectural, corporate/information services, real estate, fashion, retail industries and more!


If you need something to get from one point to another, odds are we can help!


With a track record of successfully providing solutions for outside of the box requests, you can think of our crew of experienced bikers and car drivers as mobile problem solvers.

OUR SERVICES

Our services include, but are not limited to:


  • On-demand and pre-scheduled deliveries,
    big and small
  • Rush and DIRECT ASAP services
  • Service of Process
